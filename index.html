Co-Hosts

Gagan Biyani is a technology enthusiast and entrepreneur. He is co-founder of Udemy, a leading marketplace for online education that has been featured in Bill Gates' annual letter, the New York Times, the Today Show, and more than 50 other media outlets. Prior to leading user acquisition and business operations at Udemy, Gagan wrote for TechCrunch covering mobile technology. He has also held positions at Accenture and Microsoft.

Erin Turner is an entrepreneur and product person in commerce, games, & social media. Currently she is product director at Wildfire (now a division of Google), the leading social media marketing platform. Previously Erin founded and led two startups (Crave and Gigabeat) and spent 8 years in the game industry at Electronic Arts (EA), Kixeye, and Trion Worlds.
